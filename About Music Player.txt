About Project

The objective of the Online Music Player Project is to manage the details of Music,Album,Album types etc.The project is
 totally built on administritive end and thus only the administrator is guaranteed access. The purpose of the project is to build 
an application program to reduce the manual work of management the music,performer,album.

Features of the Online Music Player are as follows:- 
-Provides the searching facilities based on various factors. Such as Music,Albums.
-Manage the information of project.
-Provides filter reports on Albums,Tracks.
-Contains the Top listed Songs that are actually Superhit in the ongoing period.
